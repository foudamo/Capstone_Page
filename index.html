WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of seven Mechatronics and Software Engineering students at McMaster University. The BPR was created as a proof of concept for a larger vision; the Autonomous Robot Platform. With this, we are hoping to prove that common tasks such as picking up and collecting objects can be made more efficient and less costly through the use of robotics.
