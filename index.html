Driving ranges are constantly filled with scattered golf balls, requiring operators to collect them regularly. This costs labour and time.



Using a robot to collect golf balls will minimize human labour, thereby reducing cost and increasing safety on a driving range.

Who are we?

We are a team of seven Mechatronics and Software Engineering students at McMaster University. The BPR was created as a proof of concept for a larger vision; the Autonomous Robot Platform. With this, we are hoping to prove that common tasks such as picking up and collecting objects can be made more efficient and less costly with the use of robotics.
